Champions

Lasercorps | Recon

* Ashe is considered an S tier unit as of this patch.
* Her high DPS ult paired with the crit damage from Recon and the extra on hit damage from Lastercorps gives gives her ample damage.
* She has security as well when paired with a second Recon unit.

Ashe

A.D.M.I.N | Brawler

* Blitzcrank is considered an S tier unit as of this patch.
* He is currently the best 1 cost tank in the game.
* His ult gives him damage reduction for the duration of the ability and is active most the game, due to how much mana he gains.
* With the Brawler trait as well, his health scales with Warmogs, which is the strongest tank item in the game currently.

Blitzcrank

Civilian | Mascot

* Galio is considered an B tier unit as of this patch.
* Galio was previously considered S tier, but with nerfs to civilian and mascot, he has become less playable
* Galio is commonly used in a Supers, Civilian, and Mascot comp.
* The scaling damage from 3 stars in Supers, paired with the Civilian mana generation, and Mascot healing optimizes Galio and his comp
* Although The combinatino of all three of those traits is great. This comp can be wildly contested and can be hard to play with no prior knowledge

Galio

Supers | Duelist

* Gangplank is considered an A tier unit as of this patch.
* Although Supers has been nerfed and Gangplank has been nerfed, he still holds up towards the top of the tiers.
* The reason why Gangplank is currently A and not a lower rank with the many nerfs is because of Duelist.
* Duelist has become a force to rekkon with in recent patches. It's scaling attack speed paired with many of the utility duelists can shred through numerous tanks
* Gangplank especially pairs very well with the scaling damage of Supers along with his ability that sheds all negative effects on him.

Gangplank

Duelist | Duelist

* Kayle is considered an A tier unit as of this patch.
* Kayle is actually a very hit or miss champion in terms of tiers. With the correct augments, items, and composition, she flourishes as an A tier.
* Without augments, BIS items, and a decent composition, she is considered D tier quite easily.
* Underground is currently a wildly loved trait. If played correctly you can acquire a Tactition's crown, Radiant items, and tons of gold.
* Her having the Underground trait is incredibly valuable. Her along with two other Underground units are very easy to find, so it can be an easy trait to splash in.

Kayle

Gadgeteens | Heart

* Lulu is considered an B tier unit as of this patch.
* Lulu is considered B tier, because she cannot put out enough damage frequently and accurately.
* You would think that her ability being able to go through multiple units would do lots of damage paired with the scalig AP of the Heart trait, would do damage.
Unfortunately It does not. Compared to other 1 costs that output lots of damage. She requires too many specific traits, specific augments, and specific items
that she is just simply not playable as a carry. She is only there to be a trait bot until better units come along.
* The Gadgeteen trait also barely does anything useful for Lulu. The items you gain off of Gadgeteen typically goes on a better AP carry or tank.

Lulu

Star-Guardian | Spellslinger

* Lux is considered an A tier unit as of this patch.
* Lux is currently considered the best 1 cost AP carry in the game. Her ult damage is incredibly high for that of a 1 cost unit.
* She can use just about any AP item, making her incredibly versatile. She also does not require any specific augment to do well.
* What sets her behind being a S tier, is her ability accuracy. Although it is an AOE ability, her accuracy is surprisingly low.
* If a unit dies where she is going to cast, she casts where the dead enemy unit is, instead of hitting a new target, making her inconsistent.

Lux

Anima-Squad | Mascot

* Nasus is considered an B tier unit as of this patch.
* Our poor Susan has struggled to fit in with the meta for quite some time now, keeping him at a consistent B tier.
* Before the Mascot nerfs pairs with his "BONK" augment, he would be considered High A tier.
* His damage output at the time paired with the healing from Mascot made him a force to be reckoned with, but only if you got that augment.
* He is entirely unplayable as a carry without the "BONK" augment, so he is simply kept out of a lot of comps, because he provides no utility or damage.

Nasus

Gadgeteens | Defender

* Poppy is considered an B tier unit as of this patch.
* Poppy has struggled to find her way into the front line tanks of 1 costs. She struggles to be a tank without so many specific items.
* Many AD carries can counter her Defender trait quite easily. Although Defender provides lots of armor, armor shredding items are prioritized in this meta.
* Even with her augment that gives 150 armor, she becomes incredibly weak to AP carries.
* The only thing preventing her from going into a lower tier, is Gadgeteen. Gadgeteen provides lots of useful tank items to help Poppy sustain.

Poppy

Lasercorps | Brawler

* Reketon is considered an A tier unit as of this patch.
* Reketon is a staple Tank in the Lastercore comp. Sejuani(4 cost Lasercorps Brawler) pairs incredibly well with Renekton.
* His ultimate gives his additional Max HP for the duration the round, making able to scale infinitely for the duration.
* When units attack him the Lasercorps drones deal damage to the unit that is attacking him, which makes him very versatile.

Renekton

Anima-Squad | Renegade

* Sylas is considered an A tier unit as of this patch.
* Sylas doesn't necessarily do a lot of damage, but as a unit he provides two very useful traits for the early and late game.
* Early Anima-Squad allows your Anima-Squad units to get the early permanent health for every unit killed, so having an early Sylas lets you scale quick.
* His second trait Renegade is very powerful in the current meta. The last Renegade alive does Bonus damage, so having 6 Renegade is very strong late.

Sylas

Ox-Force | Renegade

* Talon is considered an A tier unit as of this patch.
* Talon is such a great unit in this set. In set 8 there are no assassins.
* Talon's ability allows him to jump to a enemy unit in the corner and do lots of single target damage. This ability forces your opponent to position differently.
* With Renegade and his carry augment, he's able to cast frequently and do significantly more damage.

Talon

Mecha-Prime | Defender

* Wukong is considered an A tier unit as of this patch.
* Mecha-Prime is really what is carrying Wukong to A tier. As a carry with Mecha-Prime he does incredibly well.
* Mecha-Prime also makes him an incredible tank. It gives him a minimum of 1000 health to possibly 5000 health.
* There are also two Defenders in the Mecha-Prime trait, making Wukong a very versatile unit early, mid, and late game.

Wukong
